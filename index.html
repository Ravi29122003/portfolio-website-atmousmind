The studio

Small team. Treats your site like our own.

We started AtmousMind in 2021 because we missed the days when websites felt made, not assembled. Every project we take on is shaped by hand, by the same three or four people, from the first conversation to the last deploy.

That means no account managers, no offshore handoffs, no design that gets watered down on the way to development. You talk to the designer. You talk to the developer. Often they're the same person.

We work with founders, marketing leads, and product teams who want their website to be the best thing about their company, not the embarrassing thing they'd like to redo. We take on roughly twelve projects a year. That's the math we like.
